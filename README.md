#Beauty Retail Business Performance Analysis

## Overview
Business performance analysis for a beauty retail ecommerce company from 2021–2023 focusing on:
- revenue growth
- profitability
- promotional effectiveness
- fulfillment risk

## Tools
- PostgreSQL
- SQL
- Looker Studio

## Key Insights
- Revenue reached 60.8M with stable 38.49% profit margin during 2021-2023
- Medium discount campaigns (10–12%) achieved the best profitability balance
- Shipping delays increased during Q4 peak season
- Stockout rates peaked after year-end campaigns


## Business Recommendations
- Optimize medium-discount strategies
- Improve fulfillment scalability
- Strengthen inventory forecasting
- Reduce marketplace dependency by develop owned digital channels such as website
